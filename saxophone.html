Saxophone

For as long as I can remember, I’ve always been drawn to the saxophone. Whether it be jazz, classical, or the blues, I always love hearing the unique sound of the saxophone. I still play to this day, driven by a pure love for music. Below is a recent recording of me doing the St. Paul’s School Keiser Competition, some notable achievements, and a select repertoire history.

Notable Honors

  • 2019-2020 New Hampshire All-State Band Principal Chair Alto Saxophone
  • 2019-2020 New Hampshire All-State Saxophone Octet Principal Chair Soprano Saxophone
  • 2018-2019 St. Paul’s School Keiser Competition Second Place
  • 2017 New Jersey Region I High School Band Fourth Chair Alto Saxophone
  • 2016 New Jersey Region I Intermediate Band Fourth Chair Alto Saxophone

Ensemble Experience

  • 2018-2020 St. Paul’s School Jazz Band

  • 2016-2018 Youth Orchestra of Central Jersey Saxophone Choir
  • 2016-2018 Youth Orchestra of Central Jersey Wind Ensemble
